# Roadmap Workflow Template

This workflow helps the AI CPO build a product roadmap.

1. **Define the time horizon** – e.g., quarters or months for planning.
2. **Identify themes or initiatives** for each time period.
3. **Prioritize initiatives** based on impact and effort, using scoring frameworks.
4. **Ensure alignment** with company goals, product vision, and key metrics.
5. **Review and adjust** the roadmap regularly based on feedback, data, and changing priorities.
