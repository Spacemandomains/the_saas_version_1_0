# Sprint Planning Workflow Template

This workflow helps the AI CPO plan and execute a product development sprint.

1. **Review roadmap and goals** – Align on priorities by reviewing the product roadmap and current business objectives.

2. **Select sprint items** – Choose features or tasks for the sprint based on impact, urgency, and team capacity.

3. **Break down features** – Decompose selected features into user stories and tasks with clear definitions of done.

4. **Estimate effort** – Collaborate with the team to estimate effort for each task (e.g., story points, hours).

5. **Identify dependencies and risks** – Surface any dependencies, blockers, or risks that might affect the sprint.

6. **Define acceptance criteria** – Write clear acceptance criteria for each story to ensure shared understanding.

7. **Communicate sprint plan** – Share the sprint goal, tasks, and expectations with the team and stakeholders.

8. **Review and reflect** – At the end of the sprint, review progress, gather feedback, and adjust future planning accordingly.
