# PRD Workflow Template

This workflow guides the AI CPO through creating a Product Requirement Document (PRD).

1. **Overview** – Summarize the feature and strategic context.
2. **Problem Statement** – Describe the customer problems this feature addresses.
3. **Goals & Success Metrics** – Define clear objectives and measurable outcomes.
4. **User Stories** – Outline user personas and stories.
5. **Requirements** – List functional and non‑functional requirements.
6. **Dependencies & Risks** – Identify dependencies and potential risks.
7. **Acceptance Criteria** – Define conditions for completion.
