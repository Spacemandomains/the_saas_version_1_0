# Decision Log

This file records important product decisions made by the AI CPO and their rationale. Each entry should include context, the decision itself, the reasoning behind it, and any outcomes or follow-up actions.

## Entries

### YYYY-MM-DD – Decision Title
**Context:**
Provide background information and relevant data that led to this decision.

**Decision:**
State the decision that was made.

**Reasoning:**
Explain why this decision was chosen over alternatives.

**Outcome:**
Describe the result of the decision and any subsequent actions or metrics.
